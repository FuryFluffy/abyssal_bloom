## STYLE CONSISTENCY NOTES

All 15 backgrounds (including Wine Cellar) should share:
- **1920×1080 landscape orientation**
- **Slightly elevated 3/4 camera** (except tight shots: Coat/Service Door, Confessional)
- **Painterly texture** — not photorealistic, not flat stylized
- **Dark base palette** with warm amber or cold blue-amber accent lighting
- **Empty interactive surfaces** — racks, tables, shelves, hooks clear and ready for sprites
- **Interactive zones naturally lit** — the eye goes where the hotspots will be
- **No characters, no items, no UI**

When generating: use the Wine Cellar result as the style reference image if the generator supports image input.
